Vote for Your Preferred Golf Club < 90 Minute Edition

Shepherd's Hollow Golf Club in Clarkston is a beautiful 27-hole public golf course set among 350 acres of pristine woodlands. The Arthur Hills-designed course offers a picturesque and challenging golf experience.

The Orchards Golf Club in Washington is a public 18-hole championship course designed by Robert Trent Jones Jr. The course is well-regarded for its well-manicured fairways, fast greens, and strategic bunkering.

Tanglewood Golf Club in South Lyon features the public 27-hole "Lions, Tigers, and Bears" courses designed by William Newcomb. Each nine-hole course offers a unique challenge for golfers of all skill levels, with beautiful scenery and well-maintained grounds.

Eagle Eye Golf Club in Bath is a public 18-hole championship golf course designed by Pete Dye and Chris Lutzke. Known for its challenging layout and stunning visuals, Eagle Eye is consistently ranked among the best public courses in Michigan.

Located in Hartland, The Majestic at Lake Walden is a 27-hole public golf course designed by Jerry Matthews. Set amid beautiful hardwood forests, wetlands, and lakes, this course offers a challenging yet enjoyable golfing experience, with each nine-hole layout providing a unique test of skill.
